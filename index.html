Privacy Policy – Enchant Me!

Last updated: 22.12.2025

Privacy Policy – Enchant Me! Last updated: 22.12.2025 Enchant Me! respects your privacy. This application does not collect, store, or process personal user data. 1. Data Collection Enchant Me! does not require user registration, accounts, or login. The app does not collect: Names Email addresses Phone numbers Location data Contacts Messages Personal identifiers 2. App Functionality & Internet Usage Enchant Me! requires an internet connection to function. The app retrieves non-personal, prewritten content from publicly available sources hosted on GitHub, including: Tarot reading templates Horoscope content Moon phase information This content is downloaded when the app starts and stored temporarily in the app’s memory for use during the session. No personal information is sent or uploaded during this process. 3. Advertising Enchant Me! uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect limited device information such as: Advertising ID Device type App interaction data This data is used by Google to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, depending on the user’s device settings. Google’s advertising practices are governed by their own privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy 4. User-Stored Data The app allows users to: Save tarot readings Save sigils or spell notes Export app data to a local file on their device Import that file on another device All saved data remains entirely on the user’s device and is never uploaded to external servers. The app does not access this data except to display it locally to the user. 5. Third-Party Services The app uses the following third-party services: Google AdMob (advertising) GitHub (content hosting) No personal data is shared with GitHub. Content is fetched as static text files only. 6. Children’s Privacy Enchant Me! is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect any personal data from children. 7. Changes to This Policy This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page. 8.
